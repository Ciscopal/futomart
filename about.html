About FutoMart

The trusted campus marketplace built exclusively for FUTO community to buy and sell safely within the university community.

Our Story

FutoMart was born out of a simple observation — FUTO students needed a safe, reliable and easy way to buy and sell items within campus. From laptops and phones to textbooks and furniture, students were losing money to scammers on general platforms or missing out on great deals from fellow students nearby. FutoMart solves that by creating a trusted community marketplace just for FUTO.

How It Works

1

Create Account

Sign up with your email and create your free FutoMart account in seconds.

2

Post or Browse

List your items for sale or browse hundreds of listings from fellow students.

3

Connect & Deal

Contact sellers directly on WhatsApp and meet safely on campus to exchange.

Why FutoMart

Unlike general marketplaces, FutoMart is built specifically for FUTO students. Every seller is a verified FUTO community member. Deals happen on campus where you can inspect items before paying. No shipping, no middlemen, no hidden fees. Just students helping students.
